About World Information Architecture Day

World IA Day is a one-day conference aimed at bringing the Information Architecture community together.

On February 20th, 2016, we’ll be celebrating the fifth annual World IA Day in cities across the globe. It's a fun day to learn from world-class IA minds, network with people passionate about IA, and showcase your new IA-related ideas. It's also a fantastic opportunity to host talks with content tailored specifically to your local community and in your local language.

Sound good? We hope your city will be a host to World IA Day this year! If you have strong leadership skills and are excited to tailor World IA Day for your community, big or small, submit your application by June 20, 2015.

The basics of running World IA Day

We believe local leadership and autonomy are key to successful, culturally relevant events.

Each year, organizers run theiferently, but generally we see successful event timelines run something like this...

  1. Build a local team. You start building a team by connecting with leaders in your community who are interested in filling out the Call for Cities proposal with you. The Information Architecture Institute reviews all submissions and notifies the organizers whether or not they are accepted. Once accepted, you can grow your team and plan your event with the support of the Information Architecture Institute.
  2. Connect with global. The Information Architecture Institute global team will jump on a call with you to check in and make sure you have the support you need. We will let you know what other local leaders are doing across the globe and help you foresee any issues that can arise. We’ll also provide guidelines on when and how things could be done to help you if you’re new to event planning.
  3. Plan event logistics. We recommend a planning schedule as follows:
    July
    Recruit your volunteer team
    August
    Identify sponsors and speakers from your community
    September
    Determine venue & audio/visual needs
    October
    Publish final details on the World IA Day website
    November
    Market the event to your community
    December
    Design the day-of experience for your event
    January
    Open registration and finalize day-of details
  4. Host the event! On February 20th, 2016, you’ll host your very own World IA Day - the format and content is up to you. The day can be filled with talks, roundtable chats, workshops, or something we haven’t even thought of yet. The only thing that we ask is that you keep your event accessible to all in your community and ours. The Information Architecture Institute global team will help promote your tweets, live streams, and video uploads as your event takes place.

At each step, we will provide advice, templates, toolkits, and any other planning tools you request to make sure that you're well equipped to run your event.

Conditions for running World IA Day

The Information Architecture Institute has a few conditions cities should abide by to make sure that all World IA Day events are connected and successful.

  1. Accessible for any attendee with a desire to participate
  2. Commitment to pursuing local sponsors to cover event costs
  3. Promotion of World IA Day as an Information Architecture Institute event
  4. Promotion of global sponsors before and during the event
  5. The rights for Information Architecture Institute to publish your event content
  6. Commitment to providing the following post-event:
    • Post-event survey
    • Attendee numbers & data
    • Itemized budget
    • Any overage dollars donated to Information Architecture Institute

    7. As always, World IA Day is committed to being an open and welcoming environment. We strongly promote the inclusion of all people, regardless of gender, race, economic standing, or social background. By signing up to host World IA Day, you agree to maintain and support this standard.

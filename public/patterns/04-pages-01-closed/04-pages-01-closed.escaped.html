class="col2"> <section id="about-2016"> <h2>About 2016</h2> <p>This year, we're turning five. We're growing up!</p><p>And with growth, means change. Here are two things we're doing differently this year...</p><ul> <li><strong>We've added "Regional Directors" to our team.</strong> Areas with dense amounts of cities have been allocated a "Regional Director", in order to better align cultural interests and coordinate meetings within timezones. </li><li><strong>We're maintaining last year's style.</strong> We're spending some time reflecting on our brand this year. That means we're not changing colors nor redesigning the logo. For last year's organizers who appreciate environmental sustainability, we even recommend putting to use last year's excess printed materials.</li></ul> </section> <section id="next-steps"> <h2>About our next steps</h2> <p>There are nine months until World IA Day. Each month there will be mini-milestones for the organizers.</p><p>Here's an insight into some of our key next steps:</p><ol><li><strong>We'll select official cities.</strong> We're evaluating organizer applications and identifying next year's events. We'll announce the "official" cities soon!</li><li><strong>We'll select a theme.</strong> We are crafting ideas and will open voting in July, announcing the theme in August.</li><li><strong>We'll build our website.</strong> We're in the midst of getting a brand new World IA Day website, where people can learn more about the history of the event and where we're celebrating this year.</li></ol> </section> </div> <section id="team"> <h2>About the World IA Day Global Team</h2> <p>World IA Day is jointly produced by a network of global and regional volunteers, with support from members of the IAI Board.
